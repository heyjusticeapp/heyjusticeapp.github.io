About the app Meet the team

Introducing

Our Mission: Hey Justice empowers diverse communities with accessible, culturally fluent legal support through smart mobile first technology. Making justice not just a concept, but a tool people can use.

Our Purpose: Hey Justice bridges legal access gaps, humanizes technology, and helps diverse communities navigate the law.

Learn more
For Individuals
  • Understand legal documents in plain language
  • Track court dates with automated reminders
  • Connect with affordable lawyers
  • Access safety tools for police interactions
For Communities
  • Reduces barriers to legal representation
  • Prevents missed court dates and confusion
  • Builds confidence in justice system engagement
  • Keeps loved ones informed about safety
For Partners
  • Scale support to serve more clients efficiently
  • Extend outreach to diverse communities
  • Streamline case management processes
  • Enhance client communication and follow-up

Why Hey Justice?

Hey Justice gives people the tools, guidance, and confidence to navigate the legal system. Understand their rights, and protect themselves. We help make the law clear, accessible, and culturally fluent so everyone can face legal challenges with knowledge and support.
